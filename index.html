Program Information

Looking for a career with an innovative company that values diverse perspectives? Passionate about technology but don’t have a computer science degree?

Industry Explorers is a new program at Microsoft whose mission is to enrich diversity by embracing different perspectives. Participants come from many different industries and professions like psychology, lingusitics, chemistry, industrial engineering and child care. All, however, share a common passion for excellence.

If you would like to join this initiative, please visit our program information page to learn more.
