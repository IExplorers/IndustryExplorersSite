Program Information

At Microsoft our mission is to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more.

That’s why we launched the Industry Explorers program in the fall of 2015. Participants contribute their unique knowledge from a variety of backgrounds and industries. In return we provide time, space and resources to complete collaborative projects with engineers and program managers.

Program Specifics:
  • Three months of intense instruction and hands on projects
  • Mentorship and support from senior Microsoft engineers
  • An individual, two-month project with one of many teams
  • Competitive compensation

Our next cohort begins in February 2016.
