Program Information

The Industry Explorers Program is an immersive three-month program that provides participants real world development experience. The program combines traditional classroom learning with hands on projects which enhance the learning experience. The lecturers are led by senior engineers and are designed to enhance technical skills, or provide new content. In the second and third month of the program, candidates work on projects derived directly from our product teams.


Program Overview:

  • Three-month intense program that combines classroom instruction + hands on project
  • Mentorship and support from senior Microsoft engineers
  • Networking opportunities with hiring managers
  • Competitive compensation offered

Candidate Requirements:

  • Candidate has professional working experience
  • Candidate is looking for a career change and/or wants to re-enter the software technology industry
  • Candidates have some software programming experience
  • Must be currently authorized to work in the United States
  • Candidate must be able to pay their own relocation expenses

Application Process:

  • Resume + 1-page online application which includes three essays.
  • If selected to move forward, you will be invited to interview. There are two rounds of interviews. Reimbursement for any transportation to/from the interview is not provided.
  • Email notification of acceptance or declination is sent soon after your last interview.

Next cohort is in February 2016. Applications for the Feb 2016 being accepted from Nov 9 – Dec 9, 2016.


If you are passionate about technology and excited about this opportunity, we encourage you to apply.
